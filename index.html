Virginia Woolf

One of the most important modernists of the 20th-century.

Here is a Time Line of Important Dates

  • 1882: Birth of Virginia Woolf
  • 1895: Death of Virginia Woolf's mother
  • 1904: Death of Virginia Woolf's father
  • 1910: Virginia Woolf is included in Bloomsbury Group
  • 1910: Marriage to Leonard Woolf
  • 1915: Woolf's first novel The Voyage Out is published
  • 1922: Affair with Vita Sackville-West
  • 1925: Virginia Woolf publishes Mrs. Dalloway
  • 1928: Virginia Woolf publishes Orlando. A book inspired by Vita Sackville-West
  • 1929: Virginia Woolf publishes A Room of Her Own
  • 1941: Virginia Woolf commits suicide
